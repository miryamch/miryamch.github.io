A little bit about me

My name is Miryam, I come from Tunisia, and I am king of the hill, a number one! work as a Junior developer for a startup in Munich, Germany. I have been writing code passionately since 2006, and professionally since 2016.

Since I was a kid, I was always curious to understand how things worked. I thought studying physics would give me the universal answer, but unfortunately it didn't. On the bright side, I discovered a slightly simpler universe to grasp, that of computers.

By the end of my studies, I became fully committed to becoming a software developer, and all the learning and studying turned from a chore to a pleasure. When I graduated, I thought naively that the education phase was done. But reality quickly caught up with me, and I realized that being a developer meant never-ending learning.

The joy of ignorance

Fortunately, we live in a time where information is only a few key strokes away. I am also surrounded by an amazing team of experienced developers, which are kind enough to share their knowledge with me. Sometimes I also like to give myself a little challenge, by taking certification exams, or going to workshops on topics which scare me a little.

But this infinite amount of information can sometimes be confusing, and a little discouraging. By attending numerous conferences and Meetups on software, I gathered a decent amount of knowledge on topics which fascinated me, and I isolated those which were more pertinent for my day to day work.

Attending the Software Architecture Conference would be a very valuable experience to me, and I hope I can bring the knwolege I would gather back to my colleagues.
