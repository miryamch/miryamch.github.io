The year 2018 in Meetups

Another fact about me: I love going to Meetups and conferences, especially around software technologies and methodologies. My favorites of this year were:

  • Martin Fowler in Germany on November 27th at ThoughtWorks-Office in Munich
  • Neo4j European GraphDay, on November 20th in Munich
  • The Incredible Journey of the API on November 13th by Wirecard
  • XConf EU 2018 on June 28th by ThoughtWorks
  • Women Techmakers IWD 2018 Summit Munich March 27th, at Google
  • Tech meets HR - Fireside Chat mit Stack Overflow CEO, Joel Spolsky, on January 23rd in Munich

Each of these events allowed me to meet inspiring people, learn from their experiences, and guided me through my journey to become a better developer. My hope is to be one day on the other side of the stand and share my own experiences with others.

Meanwhile, I really hope to attend the Software Architecture Conference in New York in 2019. I know for a fact that I would learn from the best, and be even more inspired. But most importantly, I could brag about it my entire life, and make my friends and colleagues extremely jealous.
